Job Description

We are looking for a talented Senior Frontend Engineer to join our growing team. You'll be responsible for developing high-quality, scalable user interfaces and collaborating with designers and backend engineers to create outstanding products.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and maintain responsive user interfaces using React, Vue, or similar frameworks
  • Collaborate with product and design teams to translate requirements into elegant solutions
  • Implement performance optimizations and ensure cross-browser compatibility
  • Lead code reviews and maintain high code quality standards
  • Mentor junior developers and contribute to team growth
  • Stay updated with latest technologies and best practices

Required Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field
  • 5+ years of professional frontend development experience
  • Strong proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript and modern frameworks
  • Experience with HTML5, CSS3, and responsive design principles
  • Knowledge of RESTful APIs and state management solutions
  • Excellent communication and collaboration skills

Preferred Skills

  • Experience with GraphQL and server-side rendering
  • Knowledge of testing frameworks and testing best practices
  • Experience with CI/CD pipelines and deployment tools
  • Familiarity with design tools like Figma
