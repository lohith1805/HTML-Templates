About this course

This course shows how to present your skills, projects and contact details in a simple portfolio that looks serious and easy to understand.

What you'll learn

  • Plan a portfolio structure with hero, about, skills and projects.
  • Write short project descriptions that sound clear.
  • Use cards and images without making the page crowded.
  • Prepare your portfolio for sharing with recruiters or instructors.
